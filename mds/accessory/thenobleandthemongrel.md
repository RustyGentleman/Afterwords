# <Name> - Kitsune, 46M
## Backstory
Born to a family of Kitsunes of some renown, dedicated to serving the Everreach Order. Trained in combat and conjuration from an early age, he had respectable success within the guild - as Kitsunes tend to, due to their high magic affinity and tendency to be cunning and perceptive. As a Kitsune, he enjoyed ample opportunities to meet and connect with all sorts of different people, as well as plenty of excitement in new places, as well as in combat, which he drew great pleasure from.

During a mission, one of his tails was gravely wounded, leaving a permanent injury and causing him significant chronic pain. Due to this pain, he has to smoke or ingest a certain blend of herbs almost constantly. This injury also tends to make others pity him and treat him as a lesser person, which he despises. This, along with the pain, has caused him to be more cynical and overall less bright and optimistic. He's not necessarily gloomy, but he is often ill-tempered, and can be emotionally unstable at times.

## Personality
He's generally upbeat and witty, as generally expected of a Kitsune, as well as somewhat ambitious, especially regarding combat skills and honor. After the injury, however, he became more of a downer, with his wit tending to be directed towards darker and less optimistic humor more often than before.

It's not hard to pull him back into a more upbeat state, about halfway to his previous self, but it is no longer his default state. He's often still a great partyer, if in the right company, though his short temper still also makes it fairly easy to rub him the wrong way.

The greatest effect his injury had on him, however, comes from the limitations he now has where combat and hunting is concerned. Both the injury itself and the pain it causes hinder his will projection spellcasting, and he often refuses to rely on verbal and gestural casting out of spite for his condition. The event which left him injured also instilled deep inside him a lingering feeling of powerlessness, which made him simultaneously more hesitant in trusting his abilities, and, at times, more prone to being needlessly reckless and careless.

## Belongings
He has a set of tail armor pieces that go over his tails to provide some protection. They are made from light but very tough wood, woodformed by a friend of his in the Order.

He also usually carries a lugged spear and talon dagger, both Everreach standard issue.

# <Name> - ½ Kitsune, ½ Half-Wolfkin, 26F
## Backstory
Her mother, a half-Wolfkin half-Human, held some form of admiration for Wolfkin culture, but thought she wouldn't be accepted in their communities, since she was abandoned by her Wolfkin mother when still young and raised by her Human father. Her father, on the other hand, was a Kitsune, who despite not being very present, still did much to provide for her and teach her in the time they spent together.

Raised in a mixed community, her values are a mishmash of many cultures, but she does hold a particular liking for Wolfkin culture. Tutored in herbology and medicine by many over the years, she eventually became a healer herself. After this, she decided to join the Everreach Order in order to garner reputation, hopeful that it might someday help her earn the respect and kinship of a Wolfkin tribe, and the right to undergo their rites of passage.

As a member of the Order, she worked mostly as a healer, but as she was taught restoration magic, it was clear that she had an unusual ability to cast healing magic in adverse situations and under pressure. As such, she receives a fair bit of attention from her tutors and teachers.

## Personality
She's a quirky, bubbly young lady, with ample compassion and empathy, which, combined with stubbornness and general optimism, give her an almost childish demeanor.

Having become a healer as a way to hopefully earn the respect and kinship of a Wolfkin tribe someday, as well as her own interests and inclinations, she became a member of the Everreach Order to garner reputation and help others.

# Story context
Due to her ability to cast healing spells while under pressure, the reputation and renown of both <> and his family, and his emotional instability, she was appointed as his dedicated healer, as certain influential individuals were afraid he might become needlessly reckless and put himself in harm's way. As such, she is to accompany him on all missions, though upon meeting him and witnessing his downer attitude, she took it upon herself to follow him even outside of missions.
