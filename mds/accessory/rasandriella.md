## Ras & Riella's Journey

- Ras gets a message from Riella asking him to visit her

  - (Ras & Riella are close, intimate friends)

- Ras visits Ulliver telling him how to get to his home, in case he wishes to care for Ras's garden

  - (Ulliver is a fairly close friend of Ras', and owns a small apothecary and herb shop in Crestbend Harbor)

  - Ulliver's ailment is briefly mentioned (exclusive to Elfins, but he was excommunicated from his community, and refuses to return and ask to be cured on principle)

- Ras travels to the city where Riella lives

  - Small events happen during the journey

- Ras arrives at the city where Riella lives

- Riella explains that her parents have stopped returning her letters

  - (Riella ran from home as an early teenager because her parents were strict about tradition, and she didn't find it important. However, at some point, she came to understand their insistence, and began to exchange letters with them)

- Ras discovers Riella and Master Zhami were working on finding a cure for a recent disease outbreak

  - (Master Zhami is a scholar and alchemist, and currently a teacher at an important academy. Riella is under his tutelage as an alchemist)

  - They may spend some time at the city

- Ras & Riella set off towards where her parents live

  - A series of events happen during the journey

- They find out that the disease outbreak caused mail to get lost between two regions, resulting in mail not getting through.

- They arrive at the community where her parents live

- Her parents are not there, but her brother is; he tells her that they went to one of the villages that were affected by the outbreak.

- They stay until her parents come back

  - Ras discreetly requests an audience with one of the community's High Councilors and obtains information on how to cure Ulliver's disease

- When her parents come back, they explain that they found that one of their traditional rituals seems to help the sick

- Riella discovers that it isn't the ritual, but one of the components involved is an alchemical ingredient with a particular property that attacks the disease's progression

  - Riella attains a better appreciation for traditions, and the truths contained within them.

- Riella & Ras travel back to where Riella lives

- Riella brings her discovery to Master Zhami and they start working on isolating and refining the cure

- Ras travels back to the Whisperwood Forest

- Ras finds Ulliver caring for his garden

- Ras cures Ulliver of his ailment in a ritual

## Notes & Feedback:

- Explore the contrast between the Kitsune stereotype and Ras's personality, and how he copes with or challenges the stereotype.

- Focus on Ras and Riella's relationship dynamic.

- Explore (a bit) the prejudice that dark-skinned elfins suffer. Have Ras try and talk to Riella about it.

- On Ras and finding a cure for Ulliver: ensure that the resolution doesn't feel too coincidental.

- Use Ras and Riella's abilities as plot points in the mid-journey events.

- Have encounters with Elven Empire extremists harassing Riella. Have her defend herself, but also have Ras come and help.

- Have Riella look for and talk to people that might know useful properties of local materials, and gather a collection of material samples along her journey for study. Good way to establish that she's diligent in her study of alchemy, despite lacking talent.

- Have Ras gather bits of knowledge about elves and their culture, as well as hints about the legend about "talking trees" that can communicate with elves. Good way to develop his character, discovering more about his elven ancestry.

- Have other characters along the journey mention that they also stopped receiving letters and mail. Have them request Ras and Riella to deliver news or items, or try and find their loved ones.

- Riella's reunion with her parents will probably be tense, and there might be conflicts. She's open-minded and free-spirited, after all, and her parents are strict about tradition, and may be more on the prude side.
