I've always had the want to be, but an inability to. If not inability, great difficulty. But now the want is dwindling. What is left?

Does that sound like a lucid thought? Does it _sound_ like I'm simply depressed?

I have great capacity to not only dream, but to create. But, often, little power or energy to execute on that capacity. It has, so far, however, been sufficient for me to justify my being. However, I feel that is no longer the case.

Sure, it may be a matter of adapting to what is now available to me. But to be content with even less? When does there cease to be a point in it? And even if there still is a point in it, it is not a given that I will be able to adapt.
